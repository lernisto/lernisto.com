How to Get Something Done

13 April 2018 (yes, Friday)

Do you need to get something done? Something you have been procrastinating for a long time? Here is how to do it.

Choose a deadline.
In my case I chose today.
Choose a punishment.
This should be something you really don't want to do. The person I heard this from promised to give $1000 to his least favorite cousin. I offered my daughter fifty dollars, if I did not meet the deadline for posting to my blog.
Tell everyone about it.
You must make the punishment real and enforceable.
Voila
If you did all of the above correctly, selecting a reasonable deadline and a horrible punishment, you will find a way to accomplish your task.

I love you, JoyLynn, but your payday is not this week. Thank you for keeping me accountable.
